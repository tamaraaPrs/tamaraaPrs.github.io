AI Governance Reading Group

Welcome to the MILA's AI Governance Reading Group: a bi-weekly meeting to discuss AI Governance topics with researchers and students from diverse backgrounds! Each week, we alternate between discussion-based sessions (we choose a topic and a paper related to that topic, and invite everyone to read the article and come and discuss the topic) and speaker-based sessions (a speaker will come and present their research). Everyone is encouraged to join the discussion, even if you don't know much about the topic!

If you have any questions please reach out to Tamara Paris (tamara.paris@mail.mcgill.ca) or Veronica Xia (veronica.xia@mila.quebec).
