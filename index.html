PhD Student in AI Governance at McGill University

About

I am a first year PhD student in a dual PhD program at McGill University and Paris Cité, co-supervised by AJung Moon, Jin Guo, Geoffroy Couteau and Michele Orrù.

Research

My current research focuses on AI governance. I am particulary interested in AI openness and AI verifiability.
